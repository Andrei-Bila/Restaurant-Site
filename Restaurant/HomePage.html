Restaurant Description
Dave's favourites has been part of the party scene for 12 years here in London. The restaurant was open by Dave's grandfather in 1988 in Wales and later moved to London when Dave became full owner of the restaurant. Here customers can come and enjoy an elegent night samppling a selection of house root beers and enjoy some snacks alongside those. The menu here is small but a huge thought went into selecting the most decedent ingredients. A night at Dave's Favourites is definetly a night you won't forget.
